Welcome to the AI Idea Generator!

This tool offers a playful way to spark creativity and innovation. By combining a random product with a unique constraint, it challenges you to think outside the box and envision new design possibilities.

Click the "Regenerate idea" button to display a new product idea with an associated constraint. Use these prompts to kick-start your AI-related brainstorming session.

About

The generator was built by an UX-designer, without any front-end experience, only using ChatGPT-4. It was first of all an experiment to explore ChatGPT's capabilities and how a designer can aproach development with only natural language.

ChatGPT generated all HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code, managed all debugging, generated the background image, the 100 products and 50 constraints, as well as majority of the copy.
